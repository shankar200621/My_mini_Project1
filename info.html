Myths vs Facts

Myth: Mental illness is rare

Fact: 1 in 4 people will experience mental health issues at some point in their lives.

Myth: Therapy is only for severe cases

Fact: Therapy can help with everyday stress, relationships, and personal growth.

Myth: Mental health problems are a sign of weakness

Fact: Mental health issues can affect anyone, regardless of strength or resilience.

Myth: Medication is the only treatment

Fact: Many people benefit from therapy, lifestyle changes, and support networks.
